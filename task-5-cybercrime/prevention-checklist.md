# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Think before posting or sharing content online
- Avoid responding to abusive messages or trolls
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Adjust privacy settings on social media platforms
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Report and block users who engage in harassment

## Financial / UPI Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Verify payment requests before approving any transaction

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
